• Info

For Slosh was founded in 2019 as an independent project and visual narrative. Currently, the label operates with a minimalist approach serving to reflect completed ideas contributed by Tylen Slosh.


As a maker, Tylen carries particular interests in design, communication, and culture. Through exploration, For Slosh highlights the intersections of these said subjects in hopes of underlining the capabilities of timeless production.
